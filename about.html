About The Museum Arts of Cebu

The Museum Arts of Cebu preserves, researches, and exhibits Cebuano and Visayan visual culture — from pre-colonial crafts to contemporary art. We collaborate with artists, universities, and communities to celebrate the island's artistic heritage.
